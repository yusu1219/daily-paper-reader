## 摘要
生成模型在人群遗传学中发挥着重要作用，用于生成人工基因组（AG），以帮助方法基准测试、检验进化假说，以及在数据共享受限的情况下构建用于缺失填充的参考面板。然而，现有的遗传变异生成模型在保持可处理性和隐私性的同时，难以准确表达数据中的依赖关系。我们提出了遗传概率电路（Genetic Probabilistic Circuits, GPC），这是一种基于隐式 Chow-Liu 树并以概率电路表示的用于遗传变异数据的深度生成模型。GPC 泛化了传统的隐马尔可夫模型，通过允许潜在变量之间具有任意树结构，使其能够捕捉 SNP 之间的长程依赖关系。GPC 具有可处理性，支持边缘和条件概率的精确计算，从而实现人工基因组的生成和直接基因型填补（无需模拟人工基因组）。我们展示了 GPC 生成的人工基因组能够准确再现不同尺度下的人群结构和连锁不平衡模式。与其他深度生成方法相比，GPC 在填补准确度上持续提升，尤其在低频变异及公共参考面板中代表性不足的人群中表现尤为突出。最后，我们证明 GPC 更好地保护了训练数据的隐私性，从而为数据访问受限条件下的人工基因组生成提供了可行的框架。

## Abstract
Generative models play an important role in population genetics, being used to generate artificial genomes (AGs) that help benchmark methods, test evolutionary hypotheses, and construct reference panels for imputation while working around data-sharing restrictions. Existing generative models of genetic variation, however, struggle to faithfully express dependencies in the data while retaining tractability and preserving privacy. We introduce Genetic Probabilistic Circuits (GPC), a deep generative model for genetic variation data based on hidden Chow-Liu trees represented as probabilistic circuits. GPC generalizes traditional hidden Markov models by allowing arbitrary tree structures over latent variables, enabling it to capture long-range dependencies among SNPs. GPC is tractable, supporting exact computation of marginal and conditional probabilities that enables both AG generation and direct genotype imputation (avoiding the need for simulating AGs). We show that GPC generates AGs that accurately reproduce population structure and linkage disequilibrium patterns across a range of length scales. Compared to other deep generative approaches, GPC consistently improves imputation accuracy, with particularly strong gains for low-frequency variants and in populations that are not well-represented in public reference panels. Finally, we show that GPC better preserves privacy of the training data, thereby providing a practical framework for AG generation in settings with limited data access.
